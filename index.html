Mission Briefing

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Complete grammar missions aboard Nebula Station. Answer questions about Zero, First, and Second Conditionals correctly to keep the ship running!

⚡ ZERO CONDITIONAL (Blue)

Structure: If + present simple, present simple
Use: Facts, laws of nature, always-true situations
Example: If water freezes, it turns into ice.

🌟 FIRST CONDITIONAL (Green)

Structure: If + present simple, will + verb
Use: Real future possibilities
Example: If I study hard, I will pass the exam.

🔮 SECOND CONDITIONAL (Purple)

Structure: If + past simple, would + verb
Use: Imaginary / unreal situations
Example: If I had a spaceship, I would travel to Mars.

⚙️ GAME MECHANICS

• Answer correctly to earn points & XP
• Build combo streaks for bonus points
• Keep the ship energy above zero
• Wrong answers drain ship energy
• Complete all 10 questions to win!
